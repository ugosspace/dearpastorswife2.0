About the Gathering

You Don't Have to
Carry This Alone

Dear Pastor's Wife is a safe, empowering space designed specifically for pastors' wives to learn, grow, heal, connect, and be refreshed. Ministry life is beautiful — but it can also be isolating, exhausting, and deeply misunderstood by those around you.

You carry the weight of many, often in silence, often with a smile that masks everything you are truly feeling. You pour into everyone around you — the congregation, your husband, your family — and there is often no one pouring back into you.

This gathering was created because you matter. Not just as a wife. Not just as a support system. But as a woman with a calling, a voice, and a God-given purpose that is uniquely, powerfully your own. Come as you are. Leave equipped, encouraged, and deeply known.

"You were called to stand beside a great man —
but God's purpose for you is equally great."
Who Belongs Here

This Was Made for You

Wherever you are in the journey, there is a seat — and a sisterhood — waiting for you here.

The New Wife

Just beginning, finding her footing in ministry — navigating a calling she didn't fully see coming and a role with no roadmap.

The Weary One

Faithfully serving, quietly depleted — pouring into everyone around her while running on empty and longing to be poured into.

The Seasoned Leader

Years in, ready to go deeper — carrying wisdom the younger wives need and seeking fresh revelation for her next chapter.

What You'll Take Home

Clarity in Your Calling

Walk away knowing not just what you do — but why God placed you here, in this season, beside this man, for this moment in history.

Strength for the Seasons Nobody Sees

Real tools for the behind-the-scenes moments — the hospital rooms, the board crises, the quiet tears, and the unseen labor of ministry.

Healing Without Guilt

Permission to heal. Space to grieve what ministry has cost you — without shame, without judgment, and without losing your faith.

Relationships That Truly Understand

A sisterhood of women who simply get it — no explanation required. Deep, lasting bonds built on shared experience and mutual grace.

A Stronger Home, A Stronger Ministry

When you are whole, everything around you flourishes. Your home, your marriage, and your ministry all rise with you.
